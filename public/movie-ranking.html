My Top 5 Movies of all time

Cast Away

Survival Story: Tom Hanks plays a man stranded on a desert island for years.

Forrest Gump

Inspiring movie: A man with a low IQ has a successful life.

The Edge

Survival Story: A man is lost in the wilderness and must survive against all odds.

Saving Private Ryan

War movie: A group of soldiers go on a mission to find a missing soldier.

The Terminal

Drama: A man is stuck in an airport terminal and must navigate the system.
